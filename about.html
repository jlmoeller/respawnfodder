Our Guild and Alliance

Respawn Fodder and the Feeders' Anonymous Alliance are all exceedingly casual guilds that hope members will find fun, friends, and a good time.

Our active goal is to help members wherever we can, so that they can grow and exceed. We want to create a community that nurtures one another to become the best versions of their character(s), regardless of skill or builds.

We look to continue to make our alliance greater and stronger, so that we may continue our Alliance's expansion.


Our Reason for Expansion

Respawn Fodder is an Alliance Guild of Feeders' Anonymous expanding our reaches out of Central Tyria and the Heart of Magumma and placing our focus in the Crystal Desert.

Why did we decide to do this?

The answer is that we wanted to give alliance members an easy Waypoint into the Crystal Desert and have access to Hero Points, Bounties, and Exclusive Armor and Weapons from the Path of Fire content. Making this attainable for old and new characters.

To make this even easier we have established running Trains to make it easier for alliance members to develop any and all characters.


Our Stance on War

Besides aiding our Alliance in the Crystal Desert, Respawn Fodder particpates in World vs. World. While our Alliance Head, Feeders' Anonymous, makes valiant attempts on not donating kills to our adversaries. Respawn Fodder members are more than willing to lay down our lives and weaken our enemies through our mindless zombiefied invasions till we achieve victory!

We will take wins where we can. If we can not. We can not.

We only ask our members of three things: To claim land. To have fun. And most of all, to get them bags.
